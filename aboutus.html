Hello, welcome to BUGULAR STORE.

Here, Kho sells everything that Kho loves in Vintage style: from clothes, shoes and even fashion accessories. Honestly: 'What to love - who to sell!'

The journey of Vintage fashion closer to Saigon young people of Kho Nha Nhai started in 2017 with a small store, with the great passion and enthusiasm of young people who love Vintage fashion.

With the construction standard of My Warehouse is not simply 'a second-hand shop', Kho wants to tell "stories" through each set of lookbooks. With editing in product quality management, images and creating a retro shopping space, BUGULAR STORE is trying to convey the message: Vintage fashion needs a more worthy place.

Over the years, BUGULAR STORE has received a lot of love and support from loyal customers and artists. This is the motivation to always try to be better every day.

Website BUGULAR STORE is a stepping stone for the next projects of the Warehouse to continue to bring the name of My House Warehouse closer to more customers. Hopefully, this online jacket will be your new best friend. And please accompany Kho, in the arranged links for you!
